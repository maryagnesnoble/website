Some regular expression examples

Find all lines in which the word 'beak' occurs, followed by unspecified other words, and then the word 'finch'.

 beak.*finch
Explanation '.' is a wildcard that matches any character; '*' means 'repeated any number of times'. So this pattern says "search for the word 'beak', followed by any number of unspecified characters, before the word 'finch'".
